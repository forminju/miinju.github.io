**Funded by Hyundai NGV · 2024**

Proposed a zero-shot image captioning method for in-vehicle driver monitoring to generate natural language descriptions of driver states, without requiring task-specific labeled data.

**Key Contributions**
- Designed zero-shot captioning pipeline for driver state reporting
- Operated without task-specific labeled data for driver status reporting agents
- Integrated into an in-vehicle monitoring agent framework
